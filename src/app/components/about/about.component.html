About ZipCoin project

ZipCoin is a community & employee owned, operated ICO and multifunctional digital currency trading exchange platform designed to decrease existing barriers to entry in blockchain technology for all users across the globe.

ZipCoin is multicurrency and secured online platform based on blockchain technology.

ZipCoin is a unique ICO and digital trading platform that allows customers to access the exchange from the comfort of their home in fiat currencies or crypto-currencies at low fees.

ZipCoin is a multi-functional platform which allows each customer to keep money in a multi-currency online wallet, buy and sell currency (digital/fiat) on the exchange at any time and any place.
