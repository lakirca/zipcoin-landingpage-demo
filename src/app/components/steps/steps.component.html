Few steps of buying and selling digital currency

  • 01. CREATE ACCOUNT on ZIP Platform

    User creates an account with Zip. Verification can take up to 24 hours.
    OR
    User creates a trading account, or logging into an
    existing account with one of ZIP’S exchange partners (minutes).

  • 02. FUND ACCOUNT

    Users account is approved allowing the user to buy digital currency by:
    • Linking an existing banking account then wiring funds to their trading account (2-4 days) Min fees
    OR
    • Depositing cash to the ATM/Debit Card provided by ZIP to deposit cash/check/money order to their ZIP account allowing the user to trade (1 day)

  • 03. RATE | BUY | SELL DIGITAL CURRENCY

    Through ZIP's platform, the user is able to trade - buy | sell digital currency

  • 04. WITHDRAWAL

    Through ZIPs platform, the user is able to withdraw:
    • Via ATM
    • Wire fiat funds to your account
    • Wire fiat funds to another account
    • Transfer funds to another ZIP user
    • Pay bills directly
